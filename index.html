FAQ

Is microsuction safe?⌃

Yes. Microsuction is safe and gentle when performed by trained clinicians. We use magnification and sterile suction equipment.

Does it hurt?⌃

It shouldn’t. You may feel gentle suction or brief pressure. Tell us if anything is uncomfortable and we’ll pause.

Do I need to use drops beforehand?⌃

Not always. If the wax is hard or compacted, we may suggest using drops for a few days first.

What if there’s no wax to remove?⌃

We’ll examine and advise. If no wax removal is needed, we charge a £25 assessment fee.

Can children be treated?⌃

Yes, depending on age and cooperation. We’ll advise on the best approach for your child.
